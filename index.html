How to Use

  • Select a Calculator: Choose either the "Kilometers Per Liter Calculator" or "Route Fuel Consumption Calculator" from the menu.
  • Enter Data: Input the required values, such as kilometers driven, liters used, or destination distance.
  • Select Your Car: Pick your car model from the dropdown menu to ensure accurate calculations.
  • Calculate: Click the "Calculate" button to see the results.
  • Quit: Use the "Quit" button to exit the program.
